    About Us

    Dr Stat's is a survey and statistics platform where people can get paid to fill some questionnaires or publish a questionnare to collect investigation result easily.

    Our Mission

    Provide a survey platform for people to easily get to access people and collect some data. It is not just a tool, but a brand new way for prople to get their ideas and thoughts shared through the world.

    Our Plan

    Create a brand new product to save the immediate cost of making a survey, and make it possible for budget-investigators such as students or social workers to deliver a survey easily.

    Our Vision

    In the near future, it is a new time of data and information explosion. That's where we see and that's where we are going, we will be the navigator of this ship to the new future of the information sharing new era.
